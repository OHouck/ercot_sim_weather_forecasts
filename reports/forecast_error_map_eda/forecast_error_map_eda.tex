\section*{Configuration}
\begin{tabular}{ll}
\toprule
Weather variable & \texttt{temp} (temperature, °C) \\
Lead time & 1h \\
Model & HRRR \\
Months & 2025-01 to 2025-08 \\
Clusters & 9 \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}

\bigskip
For each cluster, the \textbf{treatment} map shows the hour with the
largest absolute mean temperature forecast error in that cluster.
The \textbf{control} map shows a nearby hour (within 14 days)
at the same hour of day with temperature error close to the cluster mean.
Node colors show real-time LMP (\$/MWh); the color scale is shared between
treatment and control within each cluster.
Annotations report the temperature error for that hour alongside both wind
speed and temperature errors for reference.
